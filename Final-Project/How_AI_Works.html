What is AI?

Animate

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that enables machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.

Understanding How AI Works

1. Data Collection

AI systems start by collecting large amounts of data from various sources.

2. Machine Learning

Using algorithms, the system identifies patterns and learns from the data.

3. Decision Making

The trained model makes predictions or decisions based on input data.

4. Feedback Loop

The AI system improves itself by analyzing its performance and incorporating feedback.

Real-World Examples of AI

  • Chatbots like ChatGPT
  • Recommendation systems (e.g., Netflix, Amazon)
  • Image recognition in social media
